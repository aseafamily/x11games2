🎭 How to Play Charades:

  1. One player holds the device (others see the word upside down)
  2. Act out the word WITHOUT speaking - use gestures, movements, and expressions!
  3. Team tries to guess the word as quickly as possible
  4. Tilt device DOWN when word is guessed correctly
  5. Tilt device UP to skip if too difficult
  6. Goal: Guess as many words as possible within the time limit!

📱 Device Motion Required

For tilt controls to work, we need permission to access device motion.
